Previous Baby Boks - Where Are They Now?

By: 
Sazi Luke
Thu, 12/06/2014 - 09:26

Since its inception in 2008 the SA U20 rugby side, better known as the Baby Boks, has been represented by some of the best youth rugby talent in Africa at the IRB Junior World Cup (JWC).​

Ranked amongst the best U20 rugby sides in the world, the SA junior team has managed to finish in the top three 5/6 times at the JWC, finishing 3rd four times and winning it once in 2012.

YSN took to the archives to find out where all the Baby Boks representatives are now in their pursuit of stardom and success in the senior ranks.
