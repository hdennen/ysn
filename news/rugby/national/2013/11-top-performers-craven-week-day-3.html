11 Top Performers from Craven Week Day 3

By: 
Lungelo Mdladla
Wed, 10/07/2013 - 18:15

Chris Arries
Thabani Mtsi
Jedwyn Harty
Daniel du Preez

The 2013 Coca-Cola Craven week continued on Day 3 at the Old Peter Mokaba Stadium in Polokwane.

SA schools selectors were shown great matches with the main event being the Western Province performance over the Blue Bulls as the Paarl Gim Flank and 8th man stole the show.

We Take a Look at Some Top Performing Players from Day 3:

Name Tawanda Ngosi
Position Left Wing
Team Zimbabwe
Match Played Won 45 - 3 vs Limpopo Blue Bulls

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics:

Tawanda seized counter attack opportunities for his team as he scored a try for Zimbabwe after applying pressure on a return kick from his team mate Mandivenga. Ngosi was part of most his team's backline moves and provided an attacking edge.

Name Chris Arries
Position Flyhalf
Team Namibia
Match Played Won 27 – 10 vs Griquas

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics:

The Namibian number 10 had a standout performance on the day against the Griquas, Kicking 17 points with a conversion rate of 86% (6/7) out of the total 27 points his side scored. Not only was his points kicking exceptional, but his kicking for touch put immediate pressure on the opposition throughout the game and resulted in most of the game being played in the opposition territory. Chris Arries produced an excellent all-round flyhalf game as his composure and vision made his backline very efficient and lethal, which was evident in the score line. Chris was awarded the man of the match award.

Name Thabani Sanda Mtsi
Position Prop
Team Border
Match Played Won 39 - 19 v Boland

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics:

Thabani Sanda Mtsi made a good start and a solid impact for his team as they beat Boland 39 – 19. The prop is quick on his feet, a hard runner, and has a mean hand off. Mtsi made a few breaks in the match with the most telling being his 20 metre run in the 25th minute.

Name Jedwyn Harty
Position Flank
Team Border
Match Played Won 39 - 19 v Boland

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics:

Jedwyn Harty’s hat trick try scoring performance saw him capitalize on loose balls as he made good runs inside his oppositions 22. Jedwyn played with vigour and defended well for his team.

Name Daniel & Jean-Luc du Preez
Position Flank and Lock
Team KZN
Match Played Lost 40 - 18 vs Pumas

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics:

The 2012 SA Schools du Preez brothers were relentless at the rucks and made a few steals in the match, gaining possession for their team. Daniel du Preez made a telling steal in the 17th minute and Jean Luc was cleaning well at the rucks and provided some strong attacking play. Daniel almost scored a try but his stretch over was met by an opposition boot that denied him a try. 

Names Lungelo Gosa
Position Flyhalf
Team Border
Match Played Won 39 - 19 v Boland

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics:

Gosa stepped up his game today although his day 1 performance was honourable. Gosa was a key playmaker for his team’s 39 – 19 victory. His 61st minute break where he stepped three defenders and passed to Somila Jho who finished off the move with a try was a great example of his skill. Gosa’s place kicking was also on song today and he topped his performance off with a man of the match award.  

Names Willandré Kotzenberg
Position Flank
Team Pumas
Match Played Won 40 - 18 vs KZN

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics :

 Willandré Kotzenberghad had a absolute blinder today as he constantly contested at the rucks and gained possession a few times for his team. Tristan was also reliable under the high ball and was strong in defence and in attack. His performance was topped with a try and was solid against his opposite number Jean-Luc du Preez.

Names Rikus Bothma
Position 8th man
Team Western Province
Match Played Won 30 - 6 vs Blue Bulls

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics :

Although Rikus started the half off with a dangerous tackle and was sin binned, he came back on the field and did more than make up for his absence. Bothma was defending hard at any Bulls players that ran his way. Bothma scored in the 34th minute and made a telling steal in the 58th minute. Bothma was simply a menace to the Bulls forward pack.

Names Jacques Vermeulen
Position Flank
Team Western Province
Match Played Won 30 - 6 vs Blue Bulls

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics :

Jacques worked hand in hand with Rikus Bothma as they proved to be a thorn to their counterparts. Vermeulen held up a certain Bulls try in the 33rd minute reviving his team in the process. The forward defended and played with tons of heart today and relinquished any possible try scoring opportunities from his opposition. Jacques was awarded the man of the match award.

Names Daniel du Plessis
Position Hooker
Team Western Province
Match Played Won 30 - 6 vs Blue Bulls

Match Performance Analysis & Player Characteristics :

The hooker played well all throughout the match, constantly contesting at the scrums and stealing the ball for his team. Daniel was also capable of making strong runs and defended very well against a relentless Bulls forward pack.
