School Rugby Giants Meet Again: Affies vs Grey College

By: 
Harry Dennen
Tue, 07/05/2013 - 11:20
Rudolph Snyman
EW Viljoen
Stephen Rautenbach
Eduan Keyter
Jurie Linde
Francois Steyn
Andries Kriek
Johan Venter
Marius Louw

Affies and Grey College will meet for the 37th time in history this Saturday in a match that is shaping up to be very closely contested. Both teams are brimming with talent, both teams play a disciplined game, and both teams have solid forward packs very strong in the scrum. Affies RG Snyman appears to give his side a clear advantage on line outs, though when it comes to boots Grey College holds two of the best kickers in the game right now, EW Viljoen and Sephen Rautenbach - both of whom impressed at last weekend's Wildeklawer Superskole. Grey may have the boots, but from what we've seen so far it looks like Affies might have the running game as Affies duo Eduan Keyter and Jurie Linde have demonstrated repeatedly their ability to break lines and successfully extend a phase off one another for considerable distance.

When it comes to forwards both sides look fairly even, although Affies' Francois Steyn has to be one of the best hookers playing schoolboy rugby right now with his leadership ability, carrying skill, and control in the scrum. With excellent recent performances by Adries Kriek, Johan Venter, and captain Marius Louw, Grey College may have the edge on defense. We will see how Affies recent use of eighth man Salmon van Huyssteen on the crash ball will play out in this one.

Opinions? Predictions? Let us know in the comments!
