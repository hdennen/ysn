The 9th Annual Clifton Water Polo Tournament 2013

Thursday, September 12, 2013 to Sunday, September 15, 2013
Venue: 
Durban

16 teams will take to the pool in pursuit of glory! Host's & Defending champs, Clifton, will be forced to fend off keen opposition, Reddam will look to trump their KZN rivals after losing 4 - 2 in the final.

Bronze medalist's St Johns might just be the team to beat this year, but it would be foolish to ignore the likes of Saints, up and comers Bishops, and the remaining fired up teams.
