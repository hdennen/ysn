2013 U17 CSA Cricket Week

Friday, December 6, 2013 to Tuesday, December 10, 2013
Venue: 
Western Province

 

 

Format of the Week

​Day 1: Time Cricket*
Day 2: Time Cricket*
Day 3: 2 rounds of 20/29 over games
Day 4: 50 overs
Day 5: 50 overs

*Time Cricket: At the end of the last over, the umpires will call "Time" to halt play for the day.

NB - Fixtures have been drawn up for Day One, Two and Three. Fixtures for day four and five will be done at the week.
