2013 U19 Coca-Cola Khaya Majola

Monday, December 16, 2013 to Saturday, December 21, 2013
Venue: 
Durban

The pinnacle of school boy cricket, Coca-Cola Khaya Majola kicks off in December from the 16th to the 20thin and around Durban. The SA Schools XI and SA Colts XI will be selected and will form the core of next year’s SA U19 squad.

Format of the Week

​Day 1: Time Cricket*
Day 2: Time Cricket*
Day 3: 2 rounds of 20/29 over games
Day 4: 50 overs
Day 5: 50 overs

*Time Cricket: At the end of the last over, the umpires will call "Time" to halt play for the day.

NB - Fixtures have been drawn up for Day One, Two and Three. Fixtures for day four and five will be done at the week.
