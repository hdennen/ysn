2014 CSA U15 Cricket Week

Saturday, December 13, 2014 to Wednesday, December 17, 2014
Venue: 
Oudthoorn

After primary school the competition becomes fiercer and you have to raise your game. The CSA U15 Cricket Week is the place where you can determine how great a player you can be. The first week was held in 1986 and straddles the U13 and U19 weeks. This tournament is part of a pathway from grassroots levels through to the national team and provides aspirant cricketers with an opportunity to realise their full potential. It gives you the chance to develop your budding career and to perfect your cricketing skills. The week plays a role in the transformation of the sport at school level and maintains high quality standards of play in the game.
