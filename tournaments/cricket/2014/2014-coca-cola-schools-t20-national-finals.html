2014 Coca-Cola Schools T20 National Finals

Friday, October 3, 2014 to Sunday, October 5, 2014
Venue: 
University of Pretoria

After postponement since March, the finals of the Coca-Cola national T20 festival are taking place in Pretoria this year from October 3 to 5 at LC de Villiers.

Each team will play each other as depicted in the fixture roster below.  Upon the conclusion of the preliminary round, the two teams that gain 1st and 2nd position shall contest the final.

The final log position will be determined as described in clause 21.9 points of the playing conditions.

In the event of a no result in the final, the team that finished first after the conclusion of the preliminary round shall be declared th

e winner.
