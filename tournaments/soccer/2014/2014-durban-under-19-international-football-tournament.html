2014 Durban Under-19 International Football Tournament

Monday, July 28, 2014 to Saturday, August 9, 2014
Venue: 
Durban

​

​

Six visiting nations will be represented to compete against the best SA has to offer in the under 19 age group. Foreign attending clubs are: 

  • Sporting Lisboa e Benfica, Lisbon, Portugal
  • Club Atletico Boca Juniors, Buenos Aires, Argentina
  • Everton Football Club, Liverpool, England
  • Feyenoord, Rotterdam, Holland
  • Associazione Sportiva Roma, Rome, Italy
  • Santos Futebol Clube, Sao Paulo, Brazil

“This is an overdue tournament for this age group. These are the building blocks for our future senior national team. As we work toward Moscow, these are the players to take us there." -SAFA Under 19 National Team Coach, Shakes Mashaba
