South African Schools Boat Race 2012

Thursday, December 6, 2012 to Saturday, December 8, 2012

Junior Men’s Eights race over 6,4 km
School’s boys First VIII crews excluding any current matrics.

Junior Women’s Quads race over 4 km
School’s girls First Quad crews excluding any current matrics.

Schedule

Thursday, 6 December
16h00  Registration opens at the PARSC
17h00  Coxwains taken over the course
18h00  Registration closes

Friday, 7 December
14h00 Junior Women’s time trials heads race
15h00 Junior Men’s time trials heads race

Saturday, 9 December
07h00  Start of finals
13h30  Presentation of medals at water’s edge
