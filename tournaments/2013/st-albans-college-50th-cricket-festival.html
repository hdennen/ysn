St Alban's College 50th Cricket Festival

Wednesday, February 20, 2013 to Sunday, February 24, 2013

St Alban’s College will be hosting the Independent schools cricket festival with a number of additional teams in celebration of the school's 50th anniversary.

While the festival is normally called the Independent Schools Festival, this year some traditional rivals and schools have been invited. Additional teams this year include: Maritzburg College, Affies, Pretoria Boys High, Grey Bloem, Grey PE, as well as St George's College and St John’s College from Zimbabwe.

*Pretoria Boys played two matches due to KES fixture.
