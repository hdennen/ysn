2014 Tony Stoops Rugby Festival

Saturday, March 29, 2014 to Wednesday, April 2, 2014
Venue: 
Westen Cape

Tony Stoops Rugby Festival is the biggest rugby festival in South Africa in terms of number of participants. Last year a total of 33 schools participated but this year the festival aims to get bigger by taking that number up to 40 schools.
The tournament involves schools from the Western Cape area.
